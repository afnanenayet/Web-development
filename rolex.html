Rolex: a synonym for effusive luxury

Rolex is an iconic symbol, recognized by watch enthusiasts and casual consumers alike. Rolex, a subsidiary of Tudor, has some of the best diver watches in the watch business, marked by their iconic quality and reliability.

The History

A classic Swiss watch from the beginning, Rolex has been transforming wristwatches since it was founded in 1905, in London England, by Hans Wildsorf and Alfred Davis.
